STUDY PARAMETERS
================================================================================

Ticker: BABA (NYSE)
Study Period: 2016-01-01 to 2025-12-13
Actual Data Range: 2020-07-27 to 2025-12-12 (Alpaca limitation)

Compression Detection:
  - Lookback window: N = 20 trading days (ending at day t-1)
  - range_high = max(High) over 20-day window
  - range_low = min(Low) over 20-day window
  - compression_range_pct = (range_high - range_low) / Close(t-1)

Compression Qualification:
  - Global threshold: Bottom 20th percentile of all rolling 20-day windows
  - Computed threshold: 11.77%
  - This is NOT an indicator; it's a distribution-based range contraction filter

Breakdown Trigger (Daily):
  - Day t qualifies as breakdown when:
    1) compression_range_pct <= 11.77% (bottom 20th percentile)
    2) Close(t) < range_low (breaks below compression range)

Baseline (Control Group):
  - Same compression qualification
  - BUT Close(t) >= range_low (no breakdown)

Intraday Confirmation:
  - Timeframe: 5-minute bars
  - Sessions: Premarket (3:00-8:29 CT) + RTH (8:30-15:00 CT)
  - Metrics computed:
    * Whether price traded below range_low during Premarket
    * Whether price traded below range_low during RTH
    * RTH close relative to range_low
    * Largest 5-min absolute move % and timestamp (CT)

Forward Horizons:
  - +1, +2, +3, +5, +10, +20 trading days from breakdown close
  - Metrics: Forward returns, MAE, MFE, Max Drawdown

Volatility Expansion Check:
  - Daily range % = (High - Low) / Open
  - True Range and ATR(14) from daily OHLC
  - Expansion flag: range% >= 1.25x median(prior 20-day range%)

Data Source: Alpaca (IEX feed)
Timezone: Central Time (CT)

Constraints Applied:
  ✓ Alpaca data ONLY (no other data sources)
  ✓ Central Time timezone for all timestamps/session logic
  ✓ NO INDICATORS (except ATR as volatility metric)
  ✓ No parameter tuning (N=20, 20th percentile, 1.25x multiplier per spec)
  ✓ No internet browsing
  ✓ 5-minute bars for intraday analysis

SURPRISING FINDINGS & IMPLICATIONS
================================================================================

1. **Breakdowns are Bullish, Not Bearish**
   - Conventional wisdom: breakdown below support = bearish continuation
   - BABA reality: breakdown below compression = bullish reversal
   - Median +5.00% gain at 10 days vs -0.50% for baseline

2. **Upside Skew, Not Downside**
   - Expected: breakdowns show downside asymmetry (large MAE)
   - Actual: breakdowns show UPSIDE asymmetry (MFE > |MAE|)
   - At 20 days: +11.10% MFE vs -3.99% MAE

3. **Lower Risk Than Baseline**
   - Breakdowns have smaller median drawdowns at all horizons
   - This defies the expectation that breakdowns are riskier

4. **Range Expansion is Predictive**
   - 50% of breakdowns occur with volatility expansion
   - Only 28.3% of baseline days have expansion
   - Expansion may signal conviction in the reversal

5. **Premarket Confirmation is Common**
   - 75% of breakdowns trade below range_low in premarket
   - All breakdowns confirm in RTH
   - Early breakdown may allow better entry timing

Trade Implications (if validated with more data):
- Compression breakdowns in BABA may be BUY setups, not SELL setups
- Look for range expansion as confirmation
- Premarket breakdown may offer early entry opportunity
- Risk management: median -2.47% drawdown at 20 days
- Profit target: median +3.77% at 20 days, but MFE suggests room to +11%

Caveats:
- Sample size is small (16 breakdowns)
- BABA-specific dynamics may not generalize to other tickers
- China regulatory environment creates unique mean-reversion patterns
- Results may not hold in different market regimes
