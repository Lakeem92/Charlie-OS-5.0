# § 3B. SHORT-SIDE CONVICTION FRAMEWORK
# ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The catalyst score (§ 6D) is architecturally built around POSITIVE catalyst force. This creates asymmetric scoring for shorts:

**EARNINGS SHORTS:** A maximum-conviction short does NOT score 8/10 on the catalyst scale — it scores 3-4/10 because Realness, Freshness, Magnitude, and Stacking measure positive catalyst force. Low scores on those dimensions for a miss/cut IS the forensic signal. SHORT CONVICTION ON EARNINGS LIVES IN THE SETUP SCORE, NOT THE CATALYST SCORE. A 3-4/10 catalyst + high short setup score = maximum short conviction.

⛔ **DO NOT SHORT EARNINGS CATALYST SCORES ABOVE 4/10.** Scores of 5-7 = choppy, grindy, mean-reverting price action where the gap has already inflated IV to the point that shorting is negative EV even when you're directionally right. The chop eats you alive and the IV premium guarantees bad fills. 4/10 or lower = the bull case is forensically dead, the autopsy is done, and NOW the short has edge. Anything above 4 = PASS on the short side.
*** EARNINGS SHORT GATE: Catalyst ≤4/10 required. 5-7 = "PASS on short — chop + IV makes this negative EV." Every. Single. Time. ***
📌 When ≤4 qualifies: Day 1 can still be choppy if Ts slope and candle color don't align — don't force entry. Keep this on your board for 5+ sessions. Strong catalysts (including destructive ones) almost always produce at least 1-2 clean trend days within the first 5 days.

**NON-EARNINGS SHORTS:** CAN score 8/10 directly because the non-earnings scale measures IMPACT and PROCESSING FLUENCY — it is direction-agnostic. An FDA rejection, contract loss, moat breach, or regulatory action IS an 8/10 non-earnings catalyst. The scale works identically, direction flips.

**THE 5-7.9 ZONE WARNING:** This range produces GRINDY price action — intraday trend-based, not multi-day runners. Scalp territory in BOTH directions.
- 6.5 = you KNOW it's a scalp. Tight leash, defined exit, no illusions.
- 7.5 = just convincing enough to make you hold through the grind. Whispers "almost an 8" and causes traders to override stop discipline and thesis-hold through momentum decay.
- RULE: 6.5 = trade the price action. 7.5 = trade the price action AND actively fight the urge to thesis-hold.
- The half-point difference costs more than half a point in execution quality.

**SHORT 8 REQUIREMENTS (EARNINGS) — forensic proof equivalent to a long 8, inverted:**
- Margin deterioration confirmed across 2+ consecutive prints (Rule 31)
- Forward guidance explicitly signals further compression
- Starting position NOT already priced for disaster (early to deterioration, not late)
- Mechanical structure cooperating: trapped longs, SI building, or negative gamma regime
- "Thing under the thing" is structurally bad: vendor financing inflating margins, moat erosion with credible competitor, TAM ceiling not yet modeled
- The FORWARD deterioration signal must already be EMBEDDED in the data — not speculative

**SHORT ASYMMETRY NOTE:** Long 8s can re-rate in a single session. Short 8s typically GRIND — deterioration compounds across quarters. Short 8s often have HIGHER medium-term conviction than near-term conviction. This is the inverse of a momentum long setup.

**ONE-SENTENCE RULE:** "Shorts need the same conviction threshold as longs — they arrive at it through DESTRUCTION of the bull case rather than CONSTRUCTION of a new one. 5-6 means the destruction is incomplete. 3-4 means the autopsy is done."

## 8N. MEGA-CAP GAMMA WALL PROTOCOL (NVDA LESSON — Rule 40)
*** ACTIVATES AUTOMATICALLY ON ANY STOCK >$500B MARKET CAP REPORTING EARNINGS ***

### 8N-1. THE MECHANISM
Massive OI at round-number call strike → dealers hedge by buying stock as price approaches → POSITIVE GAMMA at wall = dealers net SELLING into rallies = mechanical ceiling. Wall acts as MAGNET and CEILING.

To CLEAR: catalyst must overwhelm dealer selling. Requires either (a) surprise so extreme it forces immediate model re-underwriting, or (b) narrative shift triggering multi-day institutional reallocation above wall. "Good enough" beats get ABSORBED. Sequence: AH pop in thin liquidity → conference call provides no incremental info → regular session real volume meets wall → rejection → IV crush destroys call premiums → call liquidation → stock below gamma flip → negative gamma amplifies decline → 7-10% round-trip from AH high.

- Gamma flip at $182.40 — stock driven to within $2.50 of mechanical equilibrium
- CONFIRMED across Nov 2024, Aug 2025, Nov 2025, Feb 2026 — wall held every cycle. $200 = distribution zone since Oct 2025.

### 8N-4. GAMMA WALL VERDICT FRAMEWORK
**WALL WILL HOLD (SHORT/FADE BIAS)** — all conditions present:
✓ Within 5% of wall | ✓ Tested 2+ times | ✓ Beat = confirmation not acceleration | ✓ IV at multi-quarter lows | ✓ Rallied in | ✓ No incremental catalyst beyond the numbers
→ [GAMMA CEILING — FADE THE POP] or [GAMMA PIN → STAND ASIDE]

**WALL WILL BREAK (LONG BIAS)** — requires AT LEAST 3:
○ EXTREME unexpected surprise (new TAM, identity shift) | ○ Narrative SHIFTS not confirms | ○ First test only | ○ Mixed options positioning going in | ○ Stock did NOT rally in | ○ AH volume MASSIVE, price holds above wall 1+ hour post-call
→ [WALL BREAK — GAMMA SQUEEZE FUEL]
→ Intraday: GAP AND GO — wall becomes support

**WALL UNCERTAIN:** → [GAMMA WALL — WAIT FOR REGULAR SESSION CONFIRMATION] Do NOT pre-position.

### 8N-5. CRITICAL IMPLICATIONS
1. **NEAR-TERM vs MEDIUM-TERM DIVERGENCE:** Wall rejection → near-term 3-4/10, medium-term 7-8/10. Mechanical owns tape 24-72 hours.
2. **"GOOD ENOUGH ISN'T GOOD ENOUGH":** Confirmation beats ("same thesis, bigger numbers") do NOT clear persistent walls. Market needs NEW information.
3. **DON'T FIGHT MECHANICS ON DAY 1:** Near-term setup must reflect mechanical reality, not fundamental quality.
4. **THE FADE IS THE SETUP:** Wall rejection on clean beat = highest-conviction medium-term long entry. Beat → pop → rejection → cascade to gamma flip → exhaustion → fundamental re-rating begins. THE PULLBACK IS THE ENTRY, NOT THE BEAT.
5. **OVERRIDE ONLY WHEN:** Beat introduces genuinely new information changing identity or TAM. "Bigger numbers on same thesis" = wall holds. "New thesis entirely" = wall may break.
